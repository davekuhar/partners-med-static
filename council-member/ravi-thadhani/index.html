Ravi Thadhani, MD

Ravi Thadhani, MD, is a professor of medicine at Harvard Medical School and Chief of the Division of Nephrology at the Massachusetts General Hospital. He received his BS degree summa cum laude from The University of Notre Dame, his MD from the University of Pennsylvania, and his MPH from The Harvard School of Public Health.

Dr. Thadhani is a clinical and translational investigator with a 15-year history of funding from the National Institutes of Health (NIH). He has performed several proof-of-concept studies, observational studies, and clinical trials, both with the NIH and industry. He has experience in developing diagnostic tests, some of which are used in the clinic today.
